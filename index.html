Getting Started

Your First Month Checklist

Essential Steps (In Order)

  1. Reroll if you're going to - It's extremely helpful and fast in Uma Musume
  2. Level up cards - Focus on cards you actually use in runs, not all cards
  3. Read Basic Training Strategy - Your horse girls will thank you
  4. Add friends - Get good parents for inheritance
  5. Win every graded race once - Each first-time win gives jewels
  6. Mark good parents - Any uma with 3★ blue or 2★ blue + 3★ pink factors
  7. Spark on current banner - If you rerolled on it (200 total pulls)
  8. Do 30-50 uma rolls - Collect 1★ and 2★ umas, then save for sparks
  9. Train for each distance - Sprint, Mile, Medium, Long, and Dirt umas
  10. Join a Club - Start trading shoes for Trainer Medals

Tips for New Players

Important: Save your 3★ uma pick ticket until you finish the missions to avoid duplicates from random tickets.

Ticket Usage

If you don't have Super Creek, get Super Creek. If you do, use it to limit break one of your SSRs rather than getting a new one.

Club Benefits

Join a club ASAP. Trading shoes gives Trainer Medals for limit breaking story SSR cards. Look for clubs with many active members.

Money Management

Don't buy anything from random shops except clocks. Money is precious and most shop items aren't valuable.

Parent Strategy

Always borrow other players' umas as parents (3 times daily). Borrowed parents give +63 stats vs your own +15 stats.

Rerolling Guide

Why Reroll: Rerolling in Uma Musume is extremely fast since you don't need to redownload the game and can skip tutorials after the first time.

Rerolling Strategy

Always spend everything on Support Cards - they determine your account power. Umas are essentially cosmetic costumes with minimal gameplay impact.

Ideal Reroll Process

  1. Get banner card to 1LB+ through initial pulls
  2. Farm through runs to clear all races
  3. Get enough Carats for 200 pulls on banner
  4. Hopefully MLB the card in this process

Quick Reroll Targets

  • Super Creek - Healing skill, essential for Medium/Long
  • Tazuna - Good with few copies
  • Fine Motion - Excellent early game card
Best Start: Wait for Kitasan Black banner and reroll for 1LB, then farm to 3+LB. Kitasan MLB is like having every card in the game!

Training Priority Guidelines

Bonding Phase

  • Prioritize trainings with most support cards
  • Focus on trainings you want to level up
  • Wisdom training costs no energy
  • Hints give +5 bond per card

Rainbow Priority

  • Power rainbows > Speed rainbows in 5 Speed builds
  • Avoid single rainbows if they risk failure
  • Double fail chance rule: don't do if stats < 2x fail chance

Race Efficiency

  • G1 races: 2.86 points per energy
  • Do G1s if no strong double/triple rainbow
  • Races give stats + skill points

Motivation Management

  • 1.16x multiplier per motivation level
  • Worth dating if 6+ good training turns follow
  • Check for upcoming motivation events

Breeding Strategy

Understanding Inheritance Factors

Factor Types

  • Blue Factors (Stats): 1★ = +5, 2★ = +12, 3★ = +21 to base stats
  • Pink Factors (Aptitudes): Raise letter grades for distance/surface
  • Green/Gray Factors: Skill hints and race bonuses

Beginning Breeding Strategy

Goal: Create 9★ parents (3★ + 3★ + 3★ from self and both parents) for maximum stat inheritance (+63 stats).

Step 1: First Parent

  1. Use your 2★ factor uma + borrowed 3★ uma
  2. Aim for 2★ factor → creates 7★ uma (45 stats)
  3. Repeat until you hit 3★ factor → 8★ uma (54 stats)

Step 2: Second Parent

  1. Use your 3★ base + borrowed 3★ uma
  2. Any 2★ factor → 8★ uma
  3. 3★ factor → 9★ uma (63 stats)

Step 3: Complete Set

  1. Use two 3★ base umas together
  2. Continue until second 9★ uma
  3. Now you can breed without rentals!

3★ Factor Chances

  • Stat <600: 0% chance
  • Stat 600-1100: ~6% chance
  • Stat >1100: ~10% chance

PvP Guide

Stadium Format

How Stadium Works

  • Five 1v1 races initially, progressing to 3v3 races
  • Categories: Sprint, Mile, Medium, Long, Dirt
  • Win 3/5 categories to win the match
  • Score based on performance, not just winning
  • Need 15 unique umas eventually (no duplicates)

Champion's Meeting

Format: Monthly 3v3v3 tournament on the same track. Highly specialized umas can dominate.

Track Analysis

  • Spurt start location (Track Length ÷ 3)
  • Final corner timing
  • Hill locations and angles
  • Track-specific green skills

Strategy Considerations

  • Only need 3 umas (not 15)
  • Can duplicate strategies
  • Winning matters more than score
  • Debuffs are popular

Grade vs Open League

  • Grade: Any uma, better rewards
  • Open: B rank or worse only
  • Grade 3rd ≈ Open 1st in rewards

Key Skills by Track Type

  • Corner Spurt: Acceleration skills
  • Straight Spurt: Speed skills
  • Hills: Power-based skills
  • Weather/Season: Conditional greens

Skills Guide

Skill Categories

Green Skills (Stat Buffs)

  • Grant small stat buffs when conditions are met
  • First level: +40, Second level: +60
  • Can exceed stat caps
  • Only level once for efficiency

Blue Skills (Recovery)

  • Restore HP during races
  • Gold: 5.5% HP, White: 1.5% HP
  • Gold recoveries are essential for long distances
  • Best: Consistent timing (corner, straightaway)

Orange Skills (Speed/Acceleration)

  • Speed: +0.15-0.35 m/s for duration
  • Acceleration: +0.2-0.4 m/s/s for duration
  • Timing is crucial - spurt start acceleration is powerful
  • Duration scales with track length

Red Skills (Debuffs)

  • Affect enemy umas only
  • Speed debuffs: Always useful
  • Stamina debuffs: Powerful on longer tracks
  • All skills stack, even same names

Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I use my newly pulled uma in PvP?

You need to train them first. Pulled umas are just templates - you must complete a training run to create a racing uma.

Is this 0LB SSR card good?

Probably not. 0LB SSRs are typically worse than limit broken SRs. Check the tier lists and focus on MLB cards.

Which scenario should I play?

You only have URA scenario currently! When more release, the newest (rightmost) scenario is usually best.

Why did I lose with higher stats?

You probably got blocked. Umas can get surrounded and unable to move. Use Front Runner strategy to minimize this.

How do I win difficult races?

Most common issue is lack of Stamina. Check the Required Stamina Chart and subtract 600 for training targets.

What skills should I buy?

Pick one distance and one strategy your uma has A+ in. Buy skills ending with those tags (e.g., ＜中距離＞, ＜作戦・先行＞).

Do debuffs affect my own team?

No, debuffs only affect enemy umas. They're safe to use and won't hurt your own team members.

Do skills stack?

Yes, all skills stack. Even skills with the same name will stack their effects.

Can green skills exceed stat caps?

Yes, green skills can raise your stats to the internal cap of 2000, beyond the normal training caps.

Which SSR should I pick with tickets?

If you don't have Super Creek, get Super Creek. Otherwise, use it to limit break existing SSRs rather than getting new ones.

Common Troubleshooting
